Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join to MTC

Any high school student with a passion for technology is welcome to join the MTC Club. Whether you're a seasoned tech whiz or just starting your journey, we're here to support your growth and help you discover your potential

What happens at MTC events?

At our events, we organize workshops, tech competitions (like hackathons), and fun activities. These events help you develop skills in programming, cybersecurity, and other tech fields while enjoying your time with friends

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is a competition where teams come together to create a tech project in a limited time, like building a website or an app. It's a fun and challenging way to learn and show off your skills

What do I get from joining the club?

You'll gain tech skills, meet new friends, work on cool projects, and have fun in a friendly and creative environment. Plus, it's a great way to build your knowledge in different areas of technology!

How do I sign up?

You can join by filling out a simple form. Just ask a club member or check out our online registration, and we'll get you started right away
